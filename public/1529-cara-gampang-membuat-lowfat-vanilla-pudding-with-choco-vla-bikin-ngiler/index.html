Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang digunakan dalam pembuatan Lowfat Vanilla Pudding with Choco Vla:
  1. Ambil 1 bks Nutrijell Plain
  2. Sediakan 1 kotak susu UHT 250ml
  3. Gunakan 100 ml air
  4. Gunakan 1/2 gelas gula pasir / 5 sdm
  5. Ambil 1 bks Nutrijell Choco Vla
  6. Sediakan 200 ml air panas (utk vla)

Vla is a typical Dutch dessert cream, a kind of pudding resembling a thick custard. It is most often flavored with vanilla. Already made boxed vanilla pudding chilling in the refrigerator can be made chocolate-y in the same way. It's literally unplayable and I think Fischl would love it.

Cara membuat Lowfat Vanilla Pudding with Choco Vla:
  1. Pudding :
  2. Masukkan 1 bks nutrijell dan gula ke panci
  3. Masukkan susu dan air
  4. Masak hingga mendidih
  5. Masukkan ke cetakan setelah didiamkan 10menit
  6. Vla :
  7. Masukkan my vla nutrijell ke mangkuk
  8. Campurkan air panas, aduk hingga merata dan kental
  9. Pudding siap disajikan

Delicious and easy pudding made with vanilla and chocolate layers. It is perfect for hot summer days. This is a delicious pudding which has both vanilla and chocolate layer in them.
